How to log into AIG Connect:
Agents can access AIG Connect by visiting the intranet login page.
Enter your agent ID and password.
If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot password" link to reset it.

How to submit an application:
Go to the “New Application” section in the left-hand menu.
Choose the product, fill out the customer details, and click submit.
Ensure all required documents are uploaded.

How to check application status:
Click on “Application Tracking” in the top menu.
You can search by application ID, customer name, or date submitted.
Status will show as Pending, Approved, or Rejected.

How to contact customer support:
Use the internal messaging system or call the support center at extension 1234.
Support is available Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM.

How to reset your password:
Click the “Forgot password” link on the login page.
Follow the instructions in the email to reset your password.
If you encounter issues, contact IT support.

How to update agent profile:
Go to “My Profile” section.
Click “Edit” and update your contact information or photo.
Changes will be reviewed and applied within 24 hours.

How to upload documents:
During the application process, click the “Upload Documents” button.
Supported formats include PDF, JPG, PNG.
Make sure documents are clearly readable and under 10MB in size.

How to view commission reports:
Go to the “Reports” tab and select “Commission”.
Reports are updated monthly.
You can download reports in Excel or PDF format.
